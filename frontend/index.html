01 · Key Findings

What actually wins, and by how much

Five patterns hold robustly across all three datasets in the paper's full experiments.

FINDING A

TF-IDF beats Bag-of-Words by 3–8 percentage points across every classifier and dataset tested — the inverse-document-frequency weighting consistently earns its keep.

FINDING B

Logistic Regression is the most reliable top performer, landing at or within 0.3 points of the best score in nearly every condition, while training in under 20 seconds.

FINDING C

Bigram augmentation adds less than 1% accuracy while costing 2–3× the training time — rarely worth it outside negation-sensitive tasks.

FINDING D

kNN collapses on high-dimensional sparse vectors regardless of distance metric — the curse of dimensionality in action.

FINDING E

Naive Bayes trades a small accuracy gap for a large speed advantage — a strong lightweight default when training time matters.

FINDING F
